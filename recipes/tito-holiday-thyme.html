Tito's Holiday Thyme


Ingredients


  • 1 ½ ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
  • 1 ½ ounces cranberry juice
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, divided
  • 3 ounces sparkling water

Directions

  1. Shake Tito's Handmade Vodka, juices, and 1 sprig of thyme in a shaker with ice.

  2. Pour into a glass.

  3. Top with sparkling water and garnish with a thyme sprig.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 122 calories; carbohydrates 6.6g; sodium 4.1mg. Full Nutrition
